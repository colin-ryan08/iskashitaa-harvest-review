Tier & Role Color Guide
Foundational — entry tier, no experience needed
Experienced — needs Foundational experience + a focus skill
Specialist — needs demonstrated experience & judgment
Navy — supporting role, separate track
Red dot — safety critical · no multitasking
Amber dot — quality critical · culling decisions
Tiers describe skill, judgment, and trust level — not obligation. A volunteer may remain at any tier indefinitely; that is a complete, fully valued contribution. Numbers on each card show a suggested order within that tier only — the Harvest Lead may sequence differently based on a volunteer's interest and fit. Moving between tiers still requires demonstrated experience and Harvest Lead assessment.
