04 · Who · Visual Reference

Visual role reference

A one-page, at-a-glance map of every role across the three tiers — Foundational, Experienced, and Specialist — plus the two supporting tracks. Designed to be printed and pinned up or handed to a Harvest Lead. For the full responsibilities, prerequisites, and skills behind each role, see Roles & Expectations.

Numbers on each card show a suggested order within that tier — not a requirement. Card colors flag criticality (safety, quality, trust); tier color bands group the roles. The card below is laid out in landscape; on a narrow screen, scroll sideways within the frame, or open the full-size version to print.

The visual reference mirrors the written role definitions on Roles & Expectations. If the two ever differ, the written definitions are authoritative.
